🥘 Garlic Butter Chicken

Ready in 25 minutes | Serves 4

⏱️ Prep Time

  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 15 min

🍗 Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
📋 Full Instructions (Click to expand)
  1. Season chicken with salt & pepper
  2. Melt butter, add garlic until golden
  3. Sear chicken 6 min per side
  4. Finish with lemon juice

🥗 Nutrition

Per serving: 420 cal, Protein: 38g, Carbs: 2g
